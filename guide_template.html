When you first open the app, you will see a Setup Wizard.

  • Identity: Enter your full name (FIRST LAST) and contact details so HQ knows who you are.
  • PIN: Create a 4-digit PIN (e.g. 1234). You will need this to turn off alarms or mark yourself as safe.
  • Duress PIN: Create a secret code (e.g. 9999).

3. Locations & Visits

Syncing Locations

Your app will automatically synchronize and download any visit locations that your organisation has set for you. If new sites are added by HQ, press the "Sync" button to update your list.

Using the "Travelling" Tile

You will see a Travelling location at the top of your list.

  1. Tap the tile (it turns purple).
  2. Slide the Duration Slider to your estimated travel time.
  3. Press "Hold to Start".

How it works: The screen will turn dark to save battery. While travelling, the app sends regular GPS updates as long as the app remains open. Do not lock your screen using the power button‚Äîinstead, use the "Moon" icon üåô in the app to dim the screen safely.

Overdue Logic: If your timer runs out, you enter a "Grace Period" (%%ESCALATION_MINUTES%% minutes). If you are still overdue after this time, the alarm triggers and contacts are notified.

Mileage Reporting: When you arrive and hold "Hold to End", the app will calculate your mileage.
  • GPS Signal: Calculates actual road distance.
  • No Signal: Estimates "As the Crow Flies" distance (you can edit this manually).
  • Personal Travel? Tap the i button on the Travel tile to turn off "Report Required" for personal trips.

Adding Manual Locations

If you are visiting a new site not in the list:

  1. Tap "+ Add Manual Location".
  2. Enter a Business Name and Site Name.
  3. Tap the GPS Button to automatically fill the address, or type it manually.
  4. If your organisation requires a form on departure, select "Yes" and enter the template name (e.g. "Visit Report").

Note: You can edit or delete these manual locations later using the i button on the tile.

Location Info

Tap the i button on any tile to see contact details, gate codes, or notes. You can tap phone numbers or email addresses to contact the site directly.

4. Ending a Visit

When you are safe or have arrived:

  1. Open the app (wake the screen).
  2. Hold the Red "Hold to End" Button.
  3. If a report is required, the form will appear. Fill it out and submit to finish.

5. Vehicle Safety

Your organisation tracks vehicle safety.

  • WOF Expiry: The app knows your vehicle's WOF date. If expired, you will be blocked from starting a travel timer.
  • Vehicle Checks: You must complete a check every %%VEHICLE_INTERVAL%% days. The app will remind you when this is due.
  • To do a check manually, tap "Quick Notes" -> "Vehicle Safety Check".

6. Safety Check-ins

For high-risk work, the app will sound a chime every %%CHECKIN_INTERVAL%% minutes.

When you hear the chime, unlock your phone and hold the Green Button to confirm you are OK. Missing a check-in triggers an alarm.

7. In An Emergency

First Priority: In a life-threatening emergency, call Police/Ambulance (111/911) first.

To alert your organisation and emergency contacts:

  1. Tap the large Red SOS button 3 times rapidly.
  2. The screen will turn Red.
  3. Your GPS location is broadcast immediately to HQ and your Emergency Contact.

Cancelling an Alarm

If it was a mistake or the danger has passed:

  1. Hold the green "I AM SAFE" button.
  2. Enter your Standard PIN.
  3. This sends an "All Clear" message to your contacts.
