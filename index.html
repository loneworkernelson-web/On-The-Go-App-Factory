⏱ About 10 minutes  ·  This is the most involved step, but the instructions below walk you through it one click at a time
What is this step? Google Apps Script is a free tool that's built into your Google Spreadsheet. It acts as the "brain" of your safety system — receiving check-ins from workers, watching for overdue alarms, and sending emergency alerts. You just need to copy and paste some code into it, then click "Deploy."

1. Install the system brain

Copy the code below, then follow these steps inside your spreadsheet:

Click "Copy system code" below.
Open your spreadsheet. In the top menu, click Extensions, then Apps Script.
Delete any existing text in the editor (there's usually just a few placeholder lines — you can safely remove it all).
Paste the copied code and click the Save button (the floppy disk icon in the toolbar).
Generating...

2. Deploy the system

Still inside Apps Script, you now need to "deploy" the code so your app can talk to it. Follow these steps exactly:

Click the blue Deploy button (top right), then choose New deployment.
Click the gear icon ⚙️ next to "Select type" and choose Web app.
Set "Execute as" to Me.
Set "Who has access" to Anyone.
Click Deploy. Google may ask you to authorise the app — click through and accept.
Copy the Web App URL that appears (it starts with https://script.google.com...) and paste it in the box below.

3. Set up automatic safety checks

These scheduled tasks tell Google to check for overdue workers every 10 minutes, and to clean up private data every hour. Still inside Apps Script:

A. Overdue worker check

  1. In the left sidebar, click the clock icon (Triggers).
  2. Click + Add Trigger (bottom right).
  3. Function: checkOverdueVisits
  4. Event source: Time-driven
  5. Type: Minutes timer → Every 10 minutes
  6. Click Save.

B. Privacy clean-up

  1. Click + Add Trigger again.
  2. Function: cleanupPrivateSentNotes
  3. Event source: Time-driven
  4. Type: Hour timer → Every hour
  5. Click Save.

⏱ About 5 minutes  ·  You'll need a free Netlify account (or your own web server)
🚀

Host the Worker App

What is Netlify? Netlify is a free website hosting service. You don't need an account to get started — just drag and drop your files and you'll have a working link within seconds. You only need to create a free account to make the link permanent.
📌 Important: After Netlify gives you a link, click "Sign up to claim your site" straight away. This is free and takes about one minute. Without this, your link will stop working after one hour and your workers will be locked out.
1

Prepare your files

Extract (unzip) the AppSuite.zip file you just downloaded. Inside, find the folder named WorkerApp. This folder contains your customised app and a USER_GUIDE.html file for your staff.

2

Upload with one drag

Open Netlify Drop in a new tab. Drag the entire WorkerApp folder onto the page. Netlify will instantly give you a temporary public link.

3

Give it a professional name

Once logged in, go to Site overview → Site settings → Change site name and give it a clear, professional address — for example: yourorg-safety.netlify.app

💡 Already have your own website? If your organisation has its own web server, you can upload the WorkerApp folder there instead. This gives you full control and removes any dependency on a third-party free service.
