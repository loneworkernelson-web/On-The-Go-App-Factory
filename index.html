How to Deploy (Detailed Guide)

This creates the "Brain" of your app on Google's servers.

  1. Go to your Google Sheet: Click Extensions in the top menu, then select Apps Script.
  2. Delete any text currently in the editor. Paste the code you just copied.
  3. Click the blue Deploy button (top right) and select New Deployment.
  4. Click the "Select type" gear icon ‚öôÔ∏è and choose Web App.
  5. CRITICAL SETTINGS:
    • Description: Type v1
    • Execute as: Me (your email)
    • Who has access: Anyone (Crucial!)
  6. Click Deploy.
  7. Authorization: A popup will ask you to authorize. Click "Review Permissions".
    Note: Google will show a "Google hasn't verified this app" warning. This is normal because YOU created it.
    Click Advanced (small text) -> Go to ... (Unsafe) -> Allow.
  8. Success! Copy the Web App URL (ends in /exec) and paste it below.
‚ö†Ô∏è Vital Step: Set up Automation Triggers ‚ñº

Without triggers, the Watchdog (Safety Check) and Archiver will not run.

  1. In the Apps Script editor, click the Alarm Clock icon (Triggers) on the left sidebar.
  2. Click the blue + Add Trigger button (bottom right).
  3. Trigger 1: Safety Watchdog
    Function: checkOverdueVisits
    Event Source: Time-driven
    Type: Minutes timer -> Every 10 minutes
  4. Trigger 2: Auto-Archiver
    Function: archiveOldData
    Event Source: Time-driven
    Type: Week timer -> Every Sunday

1. Go Live (Recommended Hosting)

Host your app securely for free with Netlify.

  1. Go to app.netlify.com and Sign Up for a free account.
    Important: You must create an account first so your app doesn't expire in 24 hours.
  2. Once logged in, look for the "Sites" tab or "Add new site".
  3. Select "Deploy manually" (or drag and drop area).
  4. Find your unzipped folder on your computer.
  5. Drag the WorkerApp folder onto the Netlify page.
  6. Wait for it to upload (it takes seconds). It will give you a link (e.g., calm-badger-123.netlify.app).
  7. This is your App! Send this link to your workers.
  8. Repeat the process for the MonitorApp folder to get your Office Dashboard link.

2. Office Monitor Setup

Make the dashboard run like a real program.

Step 1: Install as App (Chrome/Edge)
  1. Open your Monitor App URL in the browser.
  2. Look at the right side of the address bar for an icon (Computer with arrow).

Step 2: Auto-Start (Windows)
  1. Press Windows Key + R, type shell:startup, and press Enter.
  2. Drag a copy of the Monitor App icon from your desktop into this folder.
